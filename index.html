The decision to adopt a dog or a cat requires correct information, for the welfare of your new pet. Regardless of whether you choose to adopt an adult dog or a cat, there are pros and cons, and the decision is a very personal one, for which you will have to inform yourself carefully and think about the type of home you will be able to offer.

Adopting a dog or a cat is an important commitment that must be weighed carefully, and if your decision is fully informed, the result can be a rewarding relationship for you and your animal.You can also get information about the dog's temperament depending on the breed and the nature of the parents. You can also ask what steps the breeder has taken to socialize the dog.

Try to see him several times before adopting him and take him out of the shelter for a few walks, both on a leash and without.
